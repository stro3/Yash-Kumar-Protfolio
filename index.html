About Me

Hey there! I am so motivated to be able to develop user-friendly, practical software. I develop applications using many different tech stacks, including but not limited to HTML/CSS/JS, Python, Java, C/C++, PHP, MongoDB and MySQL.

My primary focus is building Windows desktop applications that allow users the flexibility and power to manage their business in a simple way — whether through our inventory management system or the educational apps we create, I love taking an idea and turning it into a reliable and hassle-free solution.

Outside of writing code, I like to explore different technologies, read about new software development methodologies, and work to develop a better understanding of all aspects of the software development process.
